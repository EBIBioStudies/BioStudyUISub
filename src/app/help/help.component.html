1.Listing all your studies

The first screen shown after login is the list of so-called "Submissions". Each of these is effectively a form for the study data to be added to the BioStudies database.

There are two main categories of submissions depending on the stage they are at:

  • Pending: These are new or incomplete submission that have not been added to the BioStudies database yet.
  • Sent: These are submission already added to the BioStudies database.

Submissions of either category can be edited or deleted using the buttons with icons   under the "Actions" column.

The entire list can be filtered by any of the data columns. Just press the icon in the relevant column and provide the text or date to be filtered by. Additionally, the sorting order of the displayed rows can be changed by clicking on the icon.

2.Adding a study to the database

Depending on how experimental data is provided, there are two possible ways of adding a given study to the database. Both require clicking one of the two buttons above the list of submissions:

Creates a blank submission form to be manually filled in with study data and added to the database at your own discretion. As part of the process, the project your study belongs to must be selected. The study data required by the new form varies according to parent project.

Allows the automatic extraction of study data from a selected pre-formatted file. The data can then be incorporated into the database as a new study or used to update an existing one.

3.Editing a study

A given study can be edited at any point of its lifecycle: before being added to the database —as a "pending" submission— or after —as "sent" submission—. Additionally, in the case of a sent submission, any recent changes in the form can be cancelled, reverting the submission to its latest submitted version.

In most submission forms, some of the fields may be required, such as the study's title or its release date. If the form is submitted with any required fields blank, the application will cancel the submission process and highlight the field.

For ease of reference, all required fields are listed under the "Check" tab on the left-hand side of the form at all times. Clicking on any entry of the checklist sets the focus on the relevant field.

Please note that any submitted study will remain private and will only be accessed using login credentials until the release date.
